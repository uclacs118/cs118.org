## Course Schedule/Outline

Course content is divided into weekly modules. Due dates are communicated within each Assignment tool.

## Grade calculation

- 8% Homework
- 22% Programming projects
- 36% Two midterms
- 34% Final exam
- 0% Quizzes (quiz results will not be part of the grading)

## Technical Requirements

- Docker is required for the programming projects. Students using Windows computers will need WSL (Windows Subsystem for Linux).
- Browsers: You will need to use a browser as your primary method of accessing this course. The Canvas mobile apps should only be used as a supplement.

## Course Workload

- **Reading assignments:** Students are expected to finish the reading assignment before each lecture.
- **Homework:** Bi-weekly homework will be assigned after the 2nd lecture of Week 1, 3, 5, and 7, and due by 11:59PM Wednesday of Week 3, 5, 7, and 9. There are a total of 4 homework assignments, and one gets 2 weeks to finish each one.
- **Programming projects:** There will be 2 programming projects for this course, plus a warm-up exercise of Project-0.
- **Quizzes:** There may be a few random in-class quizzes to assess students' comprehension of the course materials. Please note that the quiz results will not be part of the course grading.
